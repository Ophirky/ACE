After consulting with Microsoft Copilot I chose for the testing part the faster model out of the three recommended models
1️⃣ vosk-model-small-en-us-0.15
- Size: 40MB
- Pros: Fast, lightweight, works on Raspberry Pi & mobile devices
- Cons: Less accurate than larger models
- Best for: Low-resource environments, quick speech recognition
2️⃣ vosk-model-en-us-0.22
- Size: 1.8GB
- Pros: High accuracy, good for general speech transcription
- Cons: Requires more memory (~16GB RAM recommended)
- Best for: Desktop/server applications needing precise transcription
3️⃣ vosk-model-en-us-0.42-gigaspeech
- Size: 2.3GB
- Pros: Best accuracy, trained on large datasets
- Cons: Heavy on resources, slower than smaller models
- Best for: Podcasts, professional dictation, high-quality speech recognition
